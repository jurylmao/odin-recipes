Cake Recipe

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 kilogram of flour
  • 12 grams of brown sugar
  • small knife for cutting hole into hell

steps

  1. Get your ingredients
  2. mix the Eggs and the Flour
  3. add brown sugar
  4. open small hole to hell
  5. place cake in hell and wait for 20 mins. Be careful not to get pulled into the hellfire yourself.
  6. pull out of hell and let cool
